About Doofy Q. Port

Practicing in All Courts of Charm & Cheer, Sworn to Uphold Article 7: Unlimited Birthday Joy

A Legacy of Legal Acumen

Doofy Q. Port, Senior Counsel and Founding Partner at Quackers & Doofy LLP. is widely regarded as a preeminent figure in the obscure yet emotionally charged realm of Affectional Law and Duck Adjacent Disputes. With over twenty years of legal excellence, Mr. Port has devoted his career to upholding the Fundamental Rights to Love, Laughter, and the Occasional Silly Goose Moment.

Since his bar admission (and bar-side brunch) in the early 2000s, Mr. Port has served as lead counsel in numerous landmark cases, including Port v. Personal Space and the internationally cited Snuggles v. Sunday Mornings. Before founding his boutique practice in 2015, he held prestigious appointments at top-tier global firms, where he routinely advised Fortune 500 partners on complex issues such as hand-holding clauses, text reply delays, and gift-overthinking liability.

A master of emotional jurisprudence, Mr. Port crafts bespoke legal strategies designed to optimize joy, maximize heart flutters, and mitigate the risk of romantic misunderstandings. His philosophy? No client too small, no cuddle clause too vague. When not in court, Mr. Port lectures on Hug Ethics, consults on high-profile birthday cases, and serves as honorary chair of the National Committee for Quackers Birthday Surprise.
